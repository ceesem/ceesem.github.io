A bit about me

Current Position

Scientist III, Allen Institute for Brain Science, Seattle, WA

Building computational infrastructure and tools and leading data analysis projects for petascale electron microscopy volumes of the nervous system.

Previous roles

  • 2012–2018: Research specialist (a.k.a. postdoc) with Albert Cardona at Janelia Research Campus, HHMI.
  • 2010–2012: Postdoc with Albert Cardona, Institute of Neuroinformatics, ETH Zürich and University of Zürich

Education

  • 2004-2010: Ph.D. in Physics, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor with Prof. Leonard Sander
  • 2000-2004: B.S., University of Washington, Seattle in Mathematics & Physics (with honors)
